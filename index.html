About Commander Enterprise BD

15 Years of Trusted Customs Excellence

Commander Enterprise BD has been a government-licensed Customs Clearing and Forwarding (C&F) agent since 2010, serving importers and exporters across Bangladesh with professionalism, modern technology, and skilled manpower.


We offer customs clearance, import/export handling, document preparation, and freight coordination — all at competitive rates, without hassle.

What We Offer

Our Services

Full-spectrum customs and freight services for importers and exporters throughout Bangladesh.

🚢
Import Clearance
Complete customs clearance for all types of imported goods — sea freight, air freight, and land port — with accurate duty assessment and fast release.
✈️
Export Clearance
Export documentation, customs filing, and shipment coordination for garments, commodities, and all export categories.
📄
Document Preparation
LC documents, Bill of Entry, Bill of Export, packing lists, commercial invoices, and all customs declarations prepared accurately.
🏭
C&F Agent Services
Licensed C&F agent representation before Bangladesh Customs for assessment, examination, and cargo release on your behalf.
📦
Freight Coordination
Coordinating with shipping lines, airlines, and trucking companies for smooth cargo movement from port to your door.
🔍
HS Code & Duty Consulting
Expert advice on correct HS code classification, applicable duty rates, VAT, AIT, and regulatory requirements for your products.
